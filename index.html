About Us

Caring for Your Loved Ones With Dignity & Love

Since 2018, Mathrushri Old Age Home has been a sanctuary for senior citizens in Bangalore. We provide a nurturing environment where elders live with dignity, joy, and the care they deserve.

7+ Years

Our Mission & Vision

We believe that every senior citizen deserves a life filled with respect, comfort, and happiness. Our mission is to provide a home away from home where elders can enjoy their golden years in peace.

Located conveniently near Vijayanagara Metro Station in Bangalore, our facility offers easy accessibility for families while providing a serene environment for our residents.

"We don't just provide care; we create a family where every elder feels valued, heard, and loved."
